Our Epoxy Colors

Our epoxy colors are no less than beautiful, rich, and superb quality. We use highly resistant and durable epoxy that is sure to withstand daily use and is easy to clean. Our epoxy is 100% solid and non-slip. Even if you find it slippery we can add an aggregate which creates a even less slipery surface. We use Polyeurea, Polyaspartic, and Standard Epoxy.
